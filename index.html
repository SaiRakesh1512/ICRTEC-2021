About The Institution

R.M.K College of Engineering and Technology is promoted by Lakshmikanthammal Educational Trust, established in the year 1995 to render philanthropic and educational service to people living in rural areas bordering Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh. The main objective of the trust is to foster quality technical and higher education, research and training in various branches of Engineering and Technology. The college is affiliated to Anna University and is certified under ISO 9001:2015 by TUVNORD, accredited by NAAC with A grade and all four programme are accredited by NBA. Our Institution is ranked --- position in NIRF ranking during the year 2020, --- position in ARIA ranking and ___ SWACHHTA 2019.

About The Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in the academic year 2008-2009. The department has highly qualified and dynamic faculty members specialized in advanced areas. The department has good infrastructure and it imparts high quality education structured to meet the present day needs. The department received AICTE - CII award for the best industry linked department. The department has extensive interactions with Professional bodies IEEE, ISTE, IET and IEI. ECE department has signed 15 MOUs for industry linkage. The department has established centre of excellence with the support of industries.

The department is regularly organizing Conferences, Symposium, training programs and workshops which will help to update the latest technologies. Many of our faculty members and students filed Patents, Published papers and completed Research and Consultancy projects.
